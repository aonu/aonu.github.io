Who are we?

Health Rhythms is an ambitious early-stage health-tech startup aimed at developing technology solutions related to patient behavior and health with a focus on reducing morbidity, improving health outcomes, and cutting overall healthcare costs.

The company is led by pioneers in technology and medicine that includes members of National Academy of Sciences Institute of Medicine, winners of prestigious TR-35 award and TED fellowship.

Our Approach

Patient centered

Brief engaging self-tracking

Passive sensing

Smartphone custom sensors to detect patterns of behavior

Personalized feedback

Actionable feedback where and when it is needed

Clinically validated

Grounded in clinical best practice & decades of research
